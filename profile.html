Hybrid system

MARS uses a hybrid approach to calculate its recommendations. The item-based filtering uses audio characteristics like danceability to deduct the similarity of your favorite albums with our recommendations. The collaborative filtering looks at the similarity of users to music review pages, for example Pitchfork. If your ratings often correlate with a specific page, the ratings of this page will be more significant to the system than others.
